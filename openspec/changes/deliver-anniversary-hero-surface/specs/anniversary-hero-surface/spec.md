## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Anniversary Hero Artifact
The system SHALL prioritize a single anniversary-grade iPod Classic hero artifact over breadth of experimental presentation modes.

#### Scenario: First Impression Priority
- **GIVEN** a user opens the product for the first time
- **WHEN** the primary interface renders
- **THEN** the experience SHALL foreground a polished iPod Classic presentation rather than a menu of equally weighted experiments
- **AND** the UI SHALL communicate that realism and tribute quality are the primary goals of this phase

### Requirement: Authenticity Over Breadth
The system SHALL prefer historically grounded realism, disciplined materials, and export readiness over adding new novelty views or controls.

#### Scenario: Scope Tradeoff
- **GIVEN** the team must choose between another experimental capability and improving the canonical artifact
- **WHEN** the choice affects this anniversary phase
- **THEN** the canonical artifact SHALL take priority
- **AND** new breadth SHALL not displace unfinished fidelity or export work
