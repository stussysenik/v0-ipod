# iPod Snapshot: Vision

## 1. Product Identity
iPod Snapshot brings nostalgia and sleek, customizable realism to your hands. It seamlessly bridges the classic era of tactical Apple design with modern, snappy web interactions. It's not just a demo; it's a "screenshot generator" where you create the perfect vibe (case, UI, track) and export it directly for sharing.

## 2. Core Value Proposition
- **Nostalgic Precision**: Perfect replica of the iPod UI, interaction flow, and aesthetics.
- **Limitless Customization**: Authentic and curated colors, backgrounds, and full hex/dropper color choices to define your moodboard.
- **Frictionless Export**: 2D images and Animated Marquee GIFs that perfectly crop and capture without leaving the browser, functioning across mobile and desktop seamlessly.

## 3. Product Principles
- **No-Lag Interactions**: The click wheel, the tap-to-edit fields, the scrubbers—all MUST feel immediate like native hardware.
- **Determinism in Action**: Exports should represent exactly what's seen. If we freeze an animation, we capture exactly that frame.
- **Mobile First, Always**: The app shouldn't penalize smaller screens. The toolbox collapses, the 3D viewer shrinks, and the layout stays tight.
