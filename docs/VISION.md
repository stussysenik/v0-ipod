# RE:MIX — Vision

RE:MIX is a museum-grade, interactive iPod Classic simulator built for the web. It recreates the full tactile experience of the 6th/7th-generation iPod Classic — the click wheel, the screen, the menu system — as a love letter to one of the most iconic consumer devices ever designed.

## Why

The iPod Classic was discontinued in 2014. Hardware degrades. Batteries swell. Hard drives fail. RE:MIX preserves the *feeling* of using one — not as a static screenshot, but as a living, interactive artifact you can hold in your browser.

## Core Beliefs

- **Fidelity over features.** Every pixel, radius, shadow, and font weight is derived from real hardware reference photos. We'd rather get one screen perfect than ship ten approximate ones.
- **Interaction is the product.** The click wheel must *feel* right — the angular momentum, the audible click, the precise threshold. Without this, it's just a picture.
- **Editability is the twist.** Unlike the original iPod, RE:MIX lets you customize the Now Playing screen — change the song title, artist, album art, rating, and time. This turns it from a simulator into a creative tool for making iPod-style mockups and exports.
- **Export as artifact.** High-fidelity PNG and GIF exports at 4x resolution let you share iPod snapshots that look indistinguishable from photos of real hardware.

## What Success Looks Like

A designer sees a RE:MIX export and asks "wait, where did you find an iPod Classic?" — then realizes it's a web app.

## Scope

### In Scope
- iPod Classic 6th Gen (2007), 120GB (2008), and Late 160GB (2009) hardware presets
- Authentic Now Playing screen with editable metadata
- iPod OS menu navigation (click wheel + center button + menu button)
- Color customization (authentic finishes + OKLCH spectrum)
- PNG export (4x) and animated GIF export (marquee text)
- 3D interactive view, focus mode, ASCII art mode
- Song snapshot save/load system

### Out of Scope (for now)
- Audio playback (visual-only simulator)
- Full iPod OS (only Menu + Now Playing screens)
- iPod Nano, Shuffle, or Touch variants
- Cloud sync or user accounts
